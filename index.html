The Problem

A candidate can interview beautifully and fall apart in the first week.

What interviews test
Confidence. Presentation. Rehearsed answers to predictable questions.
What CVs show
Where someone has been. Titles they've held. Nothing about how they actually operated.
What you're left with
A gut feeling. Three months to find out if it was right.
What the simulation captures
Priority logic. Discretion under pressure. The instinct to protect your principal's time without being told to.
What you actually see
How they triage when everything is urgent. Whether they escalate or absorb. How they write when it matters.
What you walk away with
A detailed competency report. Scored. Benchmarked. Before you've spent a single hour interviewing.
How It Works

An online simulation of a realistic EA working day.

A candidate receives a scenario brief: a company, a principal, a day that's already in motion. Emails land. Priorities collide. Stakeholders need answers. The clock is ticking. There are no "right" answers. We capture approach, judgment, composure, and communication.
